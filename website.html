Welcome to Universitas Teknokrat Indonesia

Assalamualaikum Warahmatullahi Wabarokatuh.

Welcome to official website of Universitas Teknokrat Indonesia (UTI). I am proud to introduce Universitas Teknokrat Indonesia to all readers and visitors. Established in 2017, UTI is a private university located in a southern tip of Sumatera Island, Lampung Province. UTI is non-profit institution organized by Teknokrat Education Foundation that always envision the goal to be the part of national and global education development. On this basis, Universitas Teknokrat Indonesia is here as an answer for the future leaders who eager to succeed in national and global competition.

UNIVERSITAS TEKNOKRAT INDONESI offers courses across three faculties.

bFaculty of Engineering and Computer Sciences

  • Electrical Engineering (Bachelor Program)
  • Civil Engineering (Bachelor Program)
  • Informatics (Bachelor Program)
  • Computer Engineering (Bachelor Program)
  • Information Technology (Bachelor Program)
  • Information System (Bachelor Program)
  • Accounting Information System (Diploma Program)

Faculty of Business and Economics

  • Management (Bachelor Program)
  • Accounting (Bachelor Program)

Faculty of Arts and Education

  • English Literature (Bachelor Program)
  • Mathematics Education (Bachelor Program)
  • English Education (Bachelor Program)
  • Physical Education (Bachelor Program)

Universitas Teknokrat Indonesia adopts curriculum flexible and adaptive to global challenges and development. UTI is committed to produce competent graduates equipped with professional and entrepreneurial skills.
